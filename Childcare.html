Childcare: Dos and Don'ts for Baby

Dos

  • Make sure your hands are clean before handling the baby.

  • Handle with care. Babies are very delicate.

  • Feed the baby (only when needed)

  • Change the baby's diaper when it's full

  • Make sure to change the baby's position from time to time.

  • Keep a close eye on the baby.

Don'ts

  • Do not touch the baby with dirty or contaminated hands.

  • Do not leave the baby unattended.

  • Do not make loud noises around the baby.

  • Do not play with the baby roughly.

  • Do not feed the baby solid foods (they don't have any teeth).

  • Do not wash or bathe the baby until the doctor says you can.

Note:

When a baby cries, it could signify the baby is hungry, their diaper is full, or in need of attention.
