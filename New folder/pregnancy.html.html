Pregnancy

Pregnancy is the term used when a fetus is growing in a woman’s womb. It is the condition between copulation (when the sperm meets the egg) and birth. It can last up to 40 weeks or simply 9 months.

Signs of Pregnancy

  • Fatigue

  • Missed period

  • Food craving

  • Frequent Urination

  • Breast Pain

  • Breast Changes

  • Headaches

  • Nausea

  • Darkening areolas

  • Back Pain

Tips on Taking Care of Your Pregnant Body

  • Eat healthy foods

  • Get enough sleep

  • Stay hydrated

  • Refrain from drinking alcohol

  • Get frequent check ups with your OBY-GYN

  • Get enough rest

  • Stay active through walking

  • Don't eat for two (no need for extra calories)
