About

Mother’s Memo is a site made to address the SDG Good Health and Well-being, specifically pregnant women and mothers. This site aims to spread awareness to first-time mothers and women who plan to become mothers. Its content includes information on pregnancy, tips on self-care for pregnant women, healthcare and awareness for new mothers, tips on taking care of a baby for the first time, tips for new fathers on how to support their wives and many more.

More Info on the Specified SDG

What is SDG? SDG or Sustainable Development Goals are a universal call to action to address the world’s biggest challenges. The SDG Health and Well-being or SDG 3, just like its name, aims to ensure health and well-being for all. This includes a bold commitment to end epidemics like Malaria and HIV.
